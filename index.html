Live ECG &
Symptom Logging

Our platform streams ECG data in real-time, allowing physicians to monitor patients remotely with confidence. Patient symptoms are entered digitally and automatically populate on the final report, making it immediately clear whether an event was symptomatic or asymptomatic.

  • Physicians see live rhythm data as it is acquired, with digital symptom entries tied to the timeline of the study.
  • Reports make symptomatic vs. asymptomatic context clear—without a separate handwritten diary.
  • Symptoms are matched to ECG segments on the final report for defensible documentation.
